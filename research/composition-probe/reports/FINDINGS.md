# Composition probe — findings (the last swing at fit; fit line CLOSED)

The final pre-registered test of predictive fit. **Result: HARD NULL (F20).** Trio composition/recipe
is talent-and-style in disguise — once the three forwards' individual quality and style are fixed, how
they are arranged adds no measurable, replicable performance (incremental composition CV R² = 0.04%,
below Link 2's ~1% and the 3% bar; matched balanced-minus-redundant residual CI includes zero;
complementarity coefficient negative in both eras; trio residual does not repeat within a season).
Full detail in `probe.md`; program ledger entry F20 in `research/PROGRAM-FINDINGS.md`.

## The fit line is complete (five pre-registered swings, none clearing a usability bar)
- F17 pair residual — not an identifiable persistent trait.
- F18 unit over-performance — weak, sub-bar.
- F19 behavioral dependence — real pairwise, not a stable individual trait.
- role-fit L1 roles — real & stable, but a descriptive asset, not a predictor.
- F20 composition/recipe — talent-and-style in disguise (hard null).

**Program conclusion:** predictive fit-beyond-parts does not exist at a buildable scale in public
event data. The **player is the portable unit** (his RAPM quality and his two-way role/style travel
with him). Reopening the fit question requires a **materially new data source — player tracking
(off-puck movement + passing, the Tier-iii ceiling)** — not another arrangement of public-event
features.

## Surviving assets — DESCRIPTIVE ONLY (co-occurrence, not causation)
These are stable and shippable as descriptive lineup/scouting context; **none carries a demonstrated
causal fit lift**, and each must be labeled as co-occurrence, not causation:
1. **Two-way player role profiles** (role-fit F18/L1): shot location/danger, offensive volume,
   physicality, shot-blocking, puck management — reliable within season, across seasons, and
   **player-carried** (retained ~0.79 across team changes); physicality the strongest signature.
2. **The 16 style archetypes** (this probe, Step 1): a finer style vocabulary for forwards from the
   validated two-way axes — useful for describing/brainstorming lines (5× chance-stable assignment).
3. **Shooting deference as a pairwise behavior** (dependency F19/Link A): a player's share of his
   pair's shots genuinely shifts by partner (reliability 0.35, survives the deployment+opponent
   control) — a real *descriptive* pairwise tendency, but not a stable per-player trait and not
   predictive of post-departure change.

Nothing predictive was promoted or published from the fit line. The fit question is closed pending
tracking data.
